About the Owner

Built by Discipline.
Driven by Service.

“Preparation isn't optional. It's the difference between life and death.”

Before building Johnson Performance Co., I spent years in some of the most demanding environments on earth. As a rescue swimmer, I learned that preparation isn't optional. It's the difference between life and death. Every day as a career firefighter, I rely on the same physical and mental discipline that I bring to every client session.

I built this gym, and this business, because I believe everyone deserves access to the kind of focused, expert-level training that actually changes lives. Not a cookie-cutter plan. Not a crowded gym floor. A completely private, completely custom experience built around you.

The Facility

A Private Gym.
Not a Commercial Floor.

What was once a garage is now a fully equipped luxury training studio — built from the ground up for serious, private, 1-on-1 work. This is your space when you're here. No one else.

Strength Training

Full rack, free weights, cables, and machines for complete strength programming.

Full power rack with safety bars, dual adjustable cable system, dumbbells from 5–80 lbs, adjustable bench, specialty bars, and bumper plates. Whether you're chasing your first pull-up or your next PR, every movement pattern is covered — and every program is built around your specific goals.

Cardio Equipment

Premium cardio machines for warm-ups, conditioning, and endurance work.

Rogue Echo rower, treadmill, and assault bike for everything from low-impact recovery sessions to brutal HIIT finishers. Cardio that actually serves your strength training instead of competing with it — programmed to support your goals, not random sweat for sweat's sake.

Private Sauna

Recovery is part of the process. Wind down post-session in a dedicated sauna.

A traditional cedar-wood sauna built for post-session recovery. 15–20 minutes after a hard workout improves circulation, reduces soreness, and primes your nervous system for the next session. Wind down in complete privacy — included with every session, no extra cost.

100% Private

When you're training here, this entire facility is yours. No exceptions.

From the moment you walk in, the entire facility is yours. No other clients passing through, no shared equipment, no waiting your turn. Train at your own pace, focus completely on the work, and leave knowing every minute was about you. This is what private training is supposed to feel like.
